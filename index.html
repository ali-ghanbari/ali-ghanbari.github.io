Research Interests

My research interests span the intersection of programming languages, software engineering, and data science. I use programming languages and data science tools and techniques to solve software engineering problems related to software testing, verification, and repair.

TA/RA Positions: I'm looking for Ph.D. students proficient in programming and machine learning for research in AI4SE and SE4AI. Please send your CV, if you're interested in joining my research group.

Education & Training History

On December 2021, I received my Ph.D. in Software Engineering from The University of Texas at Dallas, where I was working under the supervision of Prof. Andrian (Andi) Marcus. Previously, I received my Masters and Bachelor's degrees from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic) both in Software Engineering. From 2022 to 2023, I worked as a CIFellows Postdoctoral Research Associate at Iowa State University, under the mentorship of Prof. Hridesh Rajan.
